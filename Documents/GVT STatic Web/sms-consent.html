SMS & Text Message Consent

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

By providing your mobile phone number to Golden Valley Transit, you agree to receive text messages and SMS communications from us regarding your medical transportation services.

What Communications You'll Receive

  • Trip Confirmations: Booking confirmations with trip details, date, time, and pickup location
  • Driver Notifications: Driver name, vehicle description, and estimated arrival time
  • Appointment Reminders: Reminders about upcoming medical appointments and scheduled transportation
  • Real-Time Updates: "Driver is 10 minutes away" or "Driver has arrived" notifications
  • Account Notifications: Important updates about your account or service changes
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Security codes for accessing your online account
  • Customer Service: Responses to your inquiries and support requests

Your Consent

By providing your mobile phone number, you expressly consent to receive automated text messages from Golden Valley Transit at the phone number you provide. You understand that:

  • This consent is not required as a condition of purchasing or using our services
  • Messages may be sent using an automatic telephone dialing system
  • You may receive both transactional and informational messages
  • You can opt out at any time by following the instructions below

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your use of our services. Typical frequency:

  • Active users: 2-5 messages per week (for recurring appointments)
  • Occasional users: 1-3 messages per trip
  • Account alerts: As needed for important updates

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply as per your mobile carrier's plan. Golden Valley Transit does not charge for text messages, but your carrier may charge you for receiving texts. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

How to Opt Out (Unsubscribe)

You can stop receiving text messages at any time using any of these methods:

1

Text STOP

Reply STOP to any message from us. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. You will not receive any further messages unless you opt back in.

2

Call Us

Call our customer service at (877) 378-6368 and request to be removed from our text message list.

3

Email Us

Email us at info@goldenvalleytransit.com with "Unsubscribe from SMS" in the subject line, and include your phone number in the message.

Need Help?

For assistance or questions about text messages:

  • Reply HELP to any message from us
  • Call: (877) 378-6368
  • Email: info@goldenvalleytransit.com

Privacy & HIPAA Compliance

Your privacy is our priority. Golden Valley Transit is committed to protecting your personal and health information in compliance with HIPAA regulations:

  • We will never sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Your medical information is protected and secure
  • Text messages containing health information are encrypted and HIPAA-compliant
  • We follow strict security protocols to protect your data
  • See our full Privacy Policy for more details
