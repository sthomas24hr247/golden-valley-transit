Our Story

Serving Kern County Since 2010

Golden Valley Transit was founded with a simple mission: ensure that every patient in Kern County has reliable access to their medical appointments, regardless of their mobility challenges.

What started as a small operation with just two vehicles has grown into a trusted medical transportation provider serving thousands of patients across Bakersfield and the greater Kern County area.

Under professional medical direction, we maintain the highest standards of patient care and safety. Our team understands that medical transportation is healthcare, and we treat it with the same professionalism and compassion.
